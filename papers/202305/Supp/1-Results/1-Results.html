Introduction

This section presents 47 vectorization results generated by our method. For each example, we provide:
(1) First row: the input image and region segmentation;
(2) Second row: the reconstructed vector graphic and the decomposed vectorized layers.

Readers can zoom in on the vector graphics to see the detail. We show all layers in their stack order (i.e., lower layer on the left and higher layer on the right). Note that the reconstructed vector graphic is shown in the first column of row 2, followed by the decomposed layers generated by the Potrace library.
