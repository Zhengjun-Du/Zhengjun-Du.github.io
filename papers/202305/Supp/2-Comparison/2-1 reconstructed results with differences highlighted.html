Reconstructed results with differences highlighted

Introduction

In this page, we provide 32 results compared with Photo2ClipArt [Favreau et al. 2017]. For each comparison, we provide
1) the input image and region segmentation in row 1,
2) and the significante differences in row 2.
The Photo2ClipArt results are generated by a windows executable program provided by the authors. For each example, we try our best to generate good results with their program.

To enable users to better understand the differences of different vectorization results, we also provide an interactive page "2-2-comparison with interactive layer presentation" for user to interactively select layers to observe the composite results.
