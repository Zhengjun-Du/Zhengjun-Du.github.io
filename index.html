Zheng-Jun Du (杜正君)

Zheng-Jun Du is currently a lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Qinghai University. He got the PhD degree from Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Kun Xu. His research interests mainly include image & video processing, computer graphics, computer vision, and SLAM.
